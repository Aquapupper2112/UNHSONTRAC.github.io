About Me

This project focuses on the fabrication of microlenses to minimize optical crosstalk using cost-effective methods. These microlenses are fabricated for usage in NASA's Solar Neutron Tracking Module.

Research Focus

  • Microlens Design: Development of lens design to fit functions and parameters given by NASA.
  • Lens Design Simulation: Computer simulations of lens designs in Code V software.
  • CAD Lens Models: Lens models are available in various CAD formats for viewing.
  • SLA Lens Fabrication: Fabrication and post-processing of physical lenses utilizing stereolithography.
